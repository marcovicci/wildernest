WilderNest is about the pets.
This is a game about adopting, improving, and customizing pets, first and foremost.
Whatever other features the site has, they have to be in service of this goal.

Everyone has different ideas of what makes a virtual pet fun to care for.
To keep things tight, WilderNest can't possibly provide for all of them - at least, not now.
WilderNest pet care is broken into two different categories (so far).

Improvement: when pets can be physically trained or maybe made smarter,and compete in some way with these stats!

Customization: that's changing the look of pets, adding backdrops and cosmetic items, or even accessorizing them with mini pets.

There's some core features we're hoping to have before we launch...

  • Pets - of course. Expect a few different species, basic color-changers, and at least one special color to start with.
  • Social features - friends, boards, and private messages.
  • Games - not just to earn currency, but as monthly or weekly competitions (with trophies to show off).
  • Cosmetic equipables - like layerable background and foreground items to make your pets unique.
  • Training and contests - compete against AI components or other players in virtual pet shows.
  • Hidden features - avatars and other unlockables that you get the old fashioned way, by solving a puzzle on-site.

...and just so you know what to expect, there's some other features we aren't planning.

  • Clothes for pets - for the sake of keeping poses dynamic, don't expect fitted clothing.
  • Breeding - in order to avoid treating pets as disposable, the focus will be on improving and changing your original pets.
  • Human avatars - that art talent is be better utilized on pet-oriented art.
